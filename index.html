About FridgeFinder

Our Mission

FridgeFinder connects communities to reduce food waste and fight hunger by making surplus food easily accessible to those who need it.

Who We Are

We're a community-driven platform that helps people locate and share community fridges in their neighborhood. Our team consists of volunteers passionate about sustainability and food security.

Community Impact

  • Reduced food waste by connecting donors with recipients
  • Created a network of over 200 community fridges nationwide
  • Saved an estimated 50,000 meals from going to waste

How FridgeFinder Works

For Food Donors

  1. Find a nearby community fridge using our map
  2. Check the fridge's current needs (if listed)
  3. Bring your surplus food to the location
  4. Share what you added in the Live Updates

For Those Needing Food

  1. Locate the nearest fridge to you
  2. Check the Live Updates for recent additions
  3. Take what you need - no questions asked
  4. Consider giving back when you're able

For Fridge Hosts

  • Register your community fridge on our platform
  • Keep your listing updated with hours and needs
  • Monitor and moderate donations
  • Join our network of food security advocates
